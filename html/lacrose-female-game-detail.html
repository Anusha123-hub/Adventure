Lacrose

Lacrosse is a team sport played by two teams of ten players each. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team.

The game is played on a field with a goal at each end. The field is divided into several sections, including the attack area, the midfield line, and the defense area. Players advance the ball up the field by passing it to teammates or running with it. Once a player is in the goal area, they must shoot the ball into the goal to score a point.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Lacrosse stick

    • A stick with a netted head used to catch, carry, and shoot the ball.

  • Lacrosse ball

    • A hard rubber ball used in the game.

  • Helmet

    • A protective helmet worn to prevent head injuries.

  • Shoulder pads

    • Pads worn on the shoulders to protect against impacts.

  • Elbow pads

    • Pads worn on the elbows to protect against impacts.

  • Gloves

    • A protective helmet worn to prevent head injuries.

  • Cleats

    • Shoes with cleats for traction on the field.
