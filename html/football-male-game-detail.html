Football

The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The field is divided into two halves by the halfway line, and each half is further divided into several sections, including the penalty area, the goal area, and the center circle. Players advance the ball up the field by dribbling or passing it to teammates. Once a player stops dribbling, they must pass or shoot the ball before they can begin dribbling again. If a player moves with the ball without dribbling, it is called traveling and results in a turnover.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Football

    • A ball specifically designed for football, with a specific size and weight.

  • Football pitch

    • A rectangular field with a goal at each end, used for playing the game.

  • Football Kits

    • This typically includes a jersey, shorts, and socks. Some leagues may provide a kit, while others may require you to purchase your own.
