Athletic

Running is a popular form of exercise and sport that involves moving at a fast pace on foot. The objective of running is to cover a certain distance or complete a race in the the shortest time possible.

Running can be done on a variety of surfaces, including roads, trails, and tracks. It is important to wear proper running shoes and clothing to ensure comfort and safety. To get started in running, it is recommended to start with short distances and gradually increase the distance and intensity as you become more comfortable and confident. It is also important to warm up before running and cool down afterwards to prevent injury.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Race number

    • AA number worn on the front of the shirt to identify the runner.

  • Timing chip

    • A chip worn on the shoe or ankle to record the time of the runner.

  • Umpire's equipment

    • Whistle, counter, and protective gear for the umpires.
