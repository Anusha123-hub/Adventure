Ultimate Fisbee

Ultimate frisbee, also known as ultimate, is a team sport played by two teams of seven players each. The objective of the game is to score more points than the opposing team.

The game is played on a field with a goal at each end. The field is divided into several sections, including the end zones, the midfield line, and the sidelines. Players advance the disc up the field by passing it to teammates. Once a player is in the end zone, they must catch the disc to score a point.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Ultimate frisbee disc

    • A disc specifically designed for ultimate frisbee, with a specific size and weight.

  • Cleats

    • Shoes with cleats for traction on the field.

  • Athletic clothing

    • Comfortable clothing that allows for movement and breathability.
