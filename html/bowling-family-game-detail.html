Bowling

Bowling is a sport played by two players or teams. The objective of the game is to knock down as many pins as possible in a frame.

TTo play bowling, each player takes turns throwing the ball down the lane and knocking down the pins. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Bowling bal

    • A ball specifically designed for bowling, with a specific weight and size.

  • Bowling pins

    • Ten pins arranged in a triangle at the end of the lane.

  • Bowling shoes

    • Shoes designed specifically for bowling, with good ankle support and traction.
