Table Tennis

Table tennis, also known as ping-pong, is a sport played by two or four players on a table divided into two halves by a net. The objective of the game is to hit the ball with a racket so that it bounces on the opponent's half of the table and the opponent is unable to return it.

The game is played with a small, lightweight ball and rackets made of wood or composite materials. The ball must be hit so that it bounces on the opponent's half of the table and then on the opponent's side of the table. If the ball hits the net and then bounces on the opponent's side, it is still considered a valid shot. Players take turns serving and returning the ball, and the rally continues until one player fails

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Table tennis table

    • A rectangular table, divided into two halves by a net, used for playing the game

  • Table tennis racket

    • A racket with a short handle and a rubber-covered surface, used to hit the ball.

  • Table tennis ball

    • A small, lightweight ball, used for playing the game.
