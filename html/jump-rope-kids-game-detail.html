Jump Rope

Jump Rope is a game played with a rope. The objective of the game is to jump over the rope as it is swung under the feet.

To play Jump Rope, each player takes turns jumping over the rope as it is swung under the feet. The game ends when one player misses a jump.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Jump rope

    • A rope specifically designed for jumping, with handles at each end.

  • Jump rope surface

    • A smooth, flat surface, such as a gym floor or a sidewalk.

  • Score sheet

    • A sheet used to record the scores of the players.

  • Timer

    • A timer used to keep track of the time.
