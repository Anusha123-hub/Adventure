Basketball

Basketball is a team sport played by two teams of five players each. The objective of the game is to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.

The game is divided into four quarters, each lasting 10-12 minutes depending on the level of play. Teams are allowed to have a maximum of five players on the court at any given time, with unlimited substitutions allowed during the game. Players advance the ball up the court by dribbling or passing it to teammates. Once a player stops dribbling, they must pass or shoot the ball before they can begin dribbling again. If a player moves with the ball without dribbling, it is called traveling and results in a turnover.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Basketball

    • A ball specifically designed for basketball, with a specific size and weight.

  • Basketball hoop

    • A hoop with a net, elevated 10 feet above the ground.

  • Basketball court

    • A flat, hard surface with markings to indicate the boundaries of the court and the key.

  • Basketball shoes

    • Shoes designed specifically for basketball, with good ankle support and traction.

  • Uniform

    • This typically includes a jersey, shorts, and socks. Some leagues may provide a uniform, while others may require you to purchase your own.
