Monoply

Monopoly is a board game played by two to eight players. The objective of the game is to become the wealthiest player by buying, selling, and developing properties.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Monopoly board

    • A board with 40 spaces, including properties, Chance and Community Chest spaces, and Jail.

  • Monopoly pieces

    • Pieces used to represent the players on the board.

  • Monopoly money

    • Money used to buy and sell properties.

  • Dice

    • Two dice used to determine the number of spaces a player can move.

  • Chance and Community Chest cards

    • Cards that can affect a player's turn.

  • Properties

    • Cards representing the properties that can be bought and sold.
