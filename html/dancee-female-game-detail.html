Dance

Dance is a form of artistic expression that involves movement of the body to music. There are many different styles of dance, including ballet, hip hop, jazz, tap, and contemporary.

The objective of dance is to express oneself through movement, while also improving flexibility, strength, and coordination. Dance can be done as a form of exercise, a hobby, or a professional career. To practice dance, you will need comfortable clothing that allows for movement and breathability. It is also recommended to practice in a quiet, distraction-free space to promote focus and concentration.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Comfortable clothing

    • Clothing that allows for movement and breathability.

  • Dance shoes

    • Shoes designed specifically for dance, with good ankle support and traction.

  • Dance floor

    • A floor specifically designed for dance, with a smooth and even surface.
