Lawn Twister

Lawn Twister is a game played on a large mat with colored circles. The objective of the game is to be the last player standing.

It is also recommended to have a basic understanding of the rules of Lawn Twister, such as how to spin the spinner, how to move, and how to win the game. It is important to follow the rules and guidelines to ensure fair play and to promote a fun and engaging experience for all players.

Basic Things Needed To Play

  • Lawn Twister mat

    • A board with 40 spaces, including properties, Chance and Community Chest spaces, and Jail.A large mat with colored circles, arranged in a 4x4 grid.

  • Lawn Twister spinner

    • A spinner used to determine the color and direction of the next move.

  • Score sheet

    • A sheet used to record the scores of the players.

  • Timer

    • A timer used to keep track of the time.
